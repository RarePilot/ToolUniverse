## Classifications of Rare Diseases  
## 罕见病的分类

Orphanet nomenclature is classified by medical specialties in order to reflect the multidimensional nature of rare diseases.  
Orphanet 命名体系按医学专科进行分类，以反映罕见病的多维度特性。

Every entity can belong to multiple specialties according to their clinical presentation, and so be included in several Orphanet classifications.  
每一个疾病实体可根据其临床表现隶属于多个医学专科，因此可能同时被纳入多个 Orphanet 分类体系。

## Linearisation of Rare Diseases  
## 罕见病的线性化

The polyparental structure of the Orphanet classification of rare diseases implies that a disease is included in as many classification hierarchies as necessary depending on its clinical presentation and the medical specialties to which it is relevant.  
Orphanet 罕见病分类采用多父级结构，这意味着同一种疾病可根据其临床表现及相关医学专科，被纳入多个分类层级。

In order to enable the sorting out of all rare diseases by medical specialty and avoid multiple counting of multiclassified entities in statistical analysis, a linearisation process is applied in the Orphanet scientific knowledge base to attribute one medical specialty to each clinical entity.  
为实现按医学专科对所有罕见病进行排序，并避免在统计分析中对多重分类实体的重复计数，Orphanet 科学知识库采用线性化处理流程，为每一个临床实体指定唯一的主要医学专科。

## Phenotypes Associated with Rare Disorders  
## 与罕见病相关的表型

Orphanet rare diseases are annotated with Human Phenotype Ontology (HPO) terms, a standardised and controlled terminology covering phenotypic abnormalities in human diseases.  
Orphanet 罕见病采用人类表型本体（Human Phenotype Ontology，HPO）术语进行注释，该体系是一套覆盖人类疾病表型异常的标准化受控术语。

XML files are available in English, French, Spanish, Dutch, Portuguese, German and Italian.  
相关 XML 文件提供英文、法文、西班牙文、荷兰文、葡萄牙文、德文和意大利文版本。

## Rare Diseases and Functional Consequences  
## 罕见病及其功能后果

Orphanet rare diseases are annotated with activity limitation/participation restriction (functional consequences), using the Orphanet Functioning Thesaurus, derived and adapted from the International Classification of Functioning, Disability and Health – Children and Youth (ICF-CY, WHO 2007).  
Orphanet 罕见病在功能层面（活动受限/参与受限）进行注释，采用 Orphanet 功能词表，该词表源自并改编自《国际功能、残疾和健康分类——儿童与青少年版》（ICF-CY，WHO 2007）。

XML file is available in English, French, Spanish, Dutch, Portuguese, German and Italian.  
相关 XML 文件提供英文、法文、西班牙文、荷兰文、葡萄牙文、德文和意大利文版本。

## Natural History of Rare Diseases  
## 罕见病的自然史

The rare diseases natural history dataset includes rare diseases (ORPHAcode, preferred name, classification level, and typology) with available data related to type of inheritance, interval average age of onset.  
罕见病自然史数据集包含具有相关数据的罕见病信息，包括 ORPHAcode、首选名称、分类层级和疾病类型，以及遗传方式和平均发病年龄区间等内容。

Files are available in English, French, German, Dutch, Italian, Spanish, Portuguese, Polish and Czech.  
相关文件提供英文、法文、德文、荷兰文、意大利文、西班牙文、葡萄牙文、波兰文和捷克文版本。
